“Unveil the Secret of the Ruined City!”

Journey to the Ruined Magical City

The kingdom of Harmonia once thrived by mining a magical mineral called harmonite, which led to extraordinary advancements. However, a catastrophic event destroyed the city, forcing its people to flee and establish villages outside the city. No one knows what remains in the city now. According to rumors in the villages, harmonite is said to drive people mad and bring disaster. You play as a girl living in one of these small settlements, deeply curious about the ruined city and its enigmatic mineral. Will you uncover the secrets of Harmonia?

A World of Mysteries to Uncover

On your journey to Harmonia, you'll encounter countless experiences. Explore the world and dive into mysterious stories through your interactions with others.

  • Discover a vast, open world
  • Meet various people, help them, and listen to their stories
  • Interact with your surroundings to navigate and explore the world
